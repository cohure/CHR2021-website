Second Conference on Computational Humanities Research

November 17-19 2021, Online Conference - Program to be announced end of September.


The Computational Humanities Research (CHR) community is an international and interdisciplinary community that supports researchers with an interest in computational approaches to the humanities.

The 2021 edition of the Computational Humanities Research conference will be an online event. Check the Call for Papers!
